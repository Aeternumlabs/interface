## Prerequisites

Before you start, you need:

- A browser-based Ethereum wallet — [MetaMask](https://metamask.io), Coinbase Wallet, or any wallet supported by RainbowKit
- Sepolia test ETH — free from the faucets listed below
- A second Ethereum address to use as your backup (another wallet you control, or a fresh address from a hardware wallet)

### Getting Sepolia ETH

Sepolia test ETH is available free from two reliable faucets. Each allows one claim per day:

| Faucet | URL |
| --- | --- |
| Ethereum Sepolia Faucet | [Google Cloud Web3](https://cloud.google.com/application/web3/faucet/ethereum/sepolia) |
| Alchemy Sepolia Faucet | [sepoliafaucet.com](https://sepoliafaucet.com) |

Request at least **0.05 Sepolia ETH** — enough to cover gas fees and fund your vault during testing.

## Set Up Your Vault

<StepList>
  <StepItem step={1} title="Open the Aeternum app">
    Navigate to [aeternumvault.xyz](https://www.aeternumvault.xyz) in your browser. You will see the vault dashboard with a **Connect Wallet** prompt in the top-right corner.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem step={2} title="Connect your wallet and switch to Sepolia">
    Click **Connect Wallet**. Select your wallet from the RainbowKit modal and approve the connection request. If your wallet is not already on the Sepolia network, the app will prompt you to switch — confirm the network switch in your wallet.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem step={3} title="Register your vault">
    Click **Register** in the vault dashboard. You will be asked for two values:

    **Backup address** — the Ethereum address that receives your ETH if recovery triggers. This should be a wallet you fully control (a separate MetaMask account, a hardware wallet, or a trusted address). It cannot be your own connected address.

    **Inactivity period** — how long you need to be silent before recovery fires. For testing on Sepolia, enter **300 seconds (5 minutes)**. For real use, the minimum on mainnet is 180 days.

    Confirm the registration transaction in your wallet. The `register()` call costs a small amount of gas.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem step={4} title="Deposit test ETH into your vault">
    Once registered, click **Deposit** and enter an amount of Sepolia ETH to vault (e.g. `0.01`). Confirm the transaction. Your vault balance will update on the dashboard and the recovery countdown timer will begin.
  </StepItem>

  <StepItem step={5} title="Watch automatic recovery execute">
    With a 5-minute timer, Chainlink Automation will detect your vault as eligible roughly **5 minutes after your last interaction**. You can confirm by doing nothing — the Chainlink node calls `checkUpkeep` every 12 seconds. When it finds your vault due, it calls `performUpkeep` on-chain and your Sepolia ETH moves to your backup address automatically.

    Check your backup address balance on [Sepolia Etherscan](https://sepolia.etherscan.io) to confirm the transfer arrived.
  </StepItem>
</StepList>

<Callout type="tip" title="Reset the timer any time">
  While waiting, you can test the `Ping` button in the dashboard — it resets your countdown timer without moving any funds. This is how you would signal liveness on mainnet if you go on holiday and don't need to move funds for a while.
</Callout>
